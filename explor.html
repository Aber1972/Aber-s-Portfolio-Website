Hello! I'm Fataneh Aber

An Afghan Artist, Educator & Digital Storyteller

I am a 22-year-old Afghan woman living in the beautiful city of Herat. I was raised in a knowledge-loving family where books, creativity, and dialogue were always valued. Since childhood, I’ve had a deep passion for both visual storytelling and language education.

I'm currently a second-year student in the Graphic Design department at Herat University. I graduated from CTI’s Motion & Graphic Design program (2020–2024), where I honed my skills in creative expression, branding, and motion graphics.

Over the past three years, I’ve proudly worked as a volunteer and collaborator with World Vision. I’ve also participated in various seminars and programs, including the Promote Women’s Leadership Development initiative.
